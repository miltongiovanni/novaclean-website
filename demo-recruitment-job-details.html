Web designer

Visual and user experience aspects.

Job description

A Web Designer is responsible for creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites that align with a brand’s identity and business goals. The role involves designing page layouts, UI elements, and interactive experiences that ensure an engaging user journey across all devices.

  • About the role
  • Key responsibilities
  • Key skills

We are seeking a creative and detail-oriented Web Designer to join our team. In this role, you will be responsible for designing and improving websites that are visually engaging, user-friendly, and optimized for performance. You’ll collaborate closely with developers, marketers, and product managers to bring digital experiences to life that reflect our brand and meet business goals.

Role is to create visually engaging, user-friendly websites that bring brands to life online. You combine creativity with technical skills to design layouts, interfaces, and digital experiences that are both functional and beautiful.

As a Web Designer, you will play a vital role in shaping the visual and functional aspects of our digital presence. This section outlines the core duties and expectations for this position, highlighting the collaborative, technical, and creative contributions you'll make to deliver engaging and effective web experiences.

  • Design and maintain visually appealing and user-centered websites.
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and mockups to communicate design ideas.
  • Conduct usability testing and gather user feedback to improve designs.
  • Ensure design consistency and brand alignment across all digital platforms.
  • Stay updated on industry trends and emerging web technologies.

  • Basic knowledge of HTML/CSS (a plus).
  • Attention to detail and problem-solving mindset.
  • Ability to translate ideas into intuitive design.
